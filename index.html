About Lumière

Crafted with
intention

"We believe beautiful nails are more than a finishing touch — they're an expression of who you are."

Lumière was born from a passion for the art of nail care. Founded in 2018, our atelier has been a sanctuary where precision meets creativity. Our team of trained nail artists brings years of expertise and genuine love for their craft to every appointment.

We use only premium, cruelty-free products and maintain the highest sanitation standards. Whether you visit for a quick refresh or a full transformation, every client receives our undivided attention and care.
